About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your goal is simple: stay alive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Collect experience points, level up your character, and unlock powerful abilities to enhance your chances of survival.

With its addictive gameplay loop and strategic depth, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment as you battle to climb the leaderboards and set new personal bests. Can you survive the monster apocalypse?

Game Features

Endless Survival Action

Face wave after wave of diverse monsters with unique attack patterns and abilities. How long can you survive?

Character Progression

Level up during each run to unlock and upgrade powerful abilities that will help you survive longer against the monster hordes.

Multiple Characters

Choose from different playable characters, each with their own starting abilities and gameplay styles to master.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Your character automatically attacks nearby monsters, so focus on positioning and dodging enemy attacks. Collect the experience gems dropped by defeated monsters to level up and choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones.

As time progresses, more difficult monsters will appear, and the pace will intensify. Strategic ability choices and skillful movement are key to extending your survival time.

Tips & Strategies

Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters. Prioritize abilities that complement each other for maximum effectiveness. Don't ignore defensive upgrades - survival is just as important as dealing damage. When choosing between abilities, consider your current build and immediate threats.
